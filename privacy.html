Privacy and Safety

Overview

Welcome to our Hostel Management System. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and safety of your information. This page outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information Collection

We collect the following information during the sign-up process: username, phonenumber, email, and password. This information is necessary for account creation and hostel booking.

Data Usage

Your data is used for authentication, booking processing, and communication regarding your reservations. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent.

Data Security

We employ security measures, including secure connections, to protect your data from unauthorized access. Encryption will be added in a future update.

Cookies and Tracking

We currently do not use any cookies in our website to track user data. Cookies will be added in a future update to enhance your browsing experience.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your information. For any inquiries or requests related to your data,
please Contact us.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy, and any changes will be communicated to you through email or by posting a notice on our website. Please check this page regularly for updates.

Safety Measures

We implement safety measures in our payment processing to ensure secure transactions and protect your financial information.
