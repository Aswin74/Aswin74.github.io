About us

Project overview

HosEx is a Hostel Management project developed by our team of IT engineering students.
It aims to streamline hostel operations, making it easier for students
to manage bookings,check room availability, and enhance the overall hostel experience.

Development Process

We utilized Python and Django for the backend,
HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend,and collaborated closely
to overcome various challenges during the development process.
